Trading Strategies Overview

Simple Moving Average

Three SMA system (200, 50, 20 days) for V40 companies. Conservative approach for market leaders.

  • V40 Companies Only
  • Max 9% of portfolio
  • No averaging allowed
Applicable to: V40
V20 Strategy

20% movement pattern analysis. Buy below range, sell at range top with averaging allowed.

  • All groups applicable
  • Averaging permitted
  • No stop-loss
Applicable to: All Groups
Range-Bound Trading

Support-resistance pattern with alternating touches. Minimum 14% range size required.

  • ≥2 touches per level
  • Minimum 14% range
  • 1% entry tolerance
Applicable to: All Groups
Reverse Head & Shoulder

Classic reversal pattern for high-gain opportunities. Minimum 40% potential gain required.

  • V40 & V40 Next only
  • Minimum 40% gain
  • No stop-loss
Applicable to: V40, V40 Next
Cup with Handle

RHS pattern without left shoulder. Various cup shapes supported with complex handles.

  • V40 & V40 Next only
  • Multiple cup shapes
  • Complex handles allowed
Applicable to: V40, V40 Next
V10 Strategy

Add-on to RHS/CWH. Triggered by 10% fall after initial buy signal with reversal target.

  • Add-on strategy
  • 10% fall trigger
  • 5% gap between trades
Applicable to: V40, V40 Next
Lifetime High Strategy

"Best of the best" companies with lifetime high TTM numbers but discounted prices.

  • Lifetime high TTM
  • Max 30% below high
  • 30-40% target gain
Applicable to: V40, V40 Next
